# MomoPedia Brand Guidelines
*The definitive guide to MomoPedia's visual identity and branding*

## Brand Mission
MomoPedia is the world's most comprehensive, AI-powered encyclopedia dedicated exclusively to momos - preserving cultural heritage, celebrating regional diversity, and connecting communities through the universal love of these beloved dumplings.

## Brand Personality
- **Authentic**: Celebrating traditional recipes and cultural significance
- **Inclusive**: Embracing all momo variations from around the world  
- **Intelligent**: Powered by cutting-edge AI technology
- **Warm**: Creating a welcoming community for momo enthusiasts
- **Approachable**: Making complex culinary knowledge accessible to everyone

## Visual Identity

### Logo Concepts
The MomoPedia logo should combine:
- A stylized momo dumpling shape
- Typography that balances tradition with modernity
- Cultural elements that represent diversity
- AI/tech elements subtly integrated

### Brand Colors

#### Primary Palette
- **Momo Dough**: `#F5E6D3` - Warm, welcoming base color representing traditional dumpling dough
- **Steam White**: `#FFFFFF` - Clean, pure white representing the steam from freshly cooked momos
- **Spice Red**: `#C8102E` - Vibrant red representing chili sauces and spices
- **Wisdom Deep**: `#1B365D` - Deep blue representing knowledge, AI intelligence, and depth

#### Secondary Palette
- **Turmeric Gold**: `#E6B800` - Golden yellow representing South Asian spices
- **Fresh Herb**: `#4A7C59` - Green representing fresh ingredients and cilantro
- **Soy Dark**: `#3C2415` - Rich brown representing soy sauce and umami flavors
- **Ginger Warm**: `#D4A574` - Warm tan representing ginger and garlic

#### Accent Colors
- **Chili Hot**: `#FF4444` - Bright red for notifications, alerts, and call-to-actions
- **Mint Cool**: `#66D9A3` - Cool green for success states and confirmations
- **Saffron Highlight**: `#FF8C42` - Orange for highlights and interactive elements
- **Lavender Soft**: `#B19CD9` - Soft purple for AI/tech elements

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Modern, clean, excellent readability)
- **Secondary Font**: Noto Sans (International character support for multilingual content)
- **Accent Font**: Playfair Display (For elegant headlines and cultural emphasis)

### Design Principles
1. **Cultural Sensitivity**: Always respect the cultural origins of each momo variety
2. **Accessibility**: Ensure all content is accessible across devices and abilities
3. **Consistency**: Maintain visual consistency across all platforms
4. **Warmth**: Use rounded corners, warm colors, and friendly interactions
5. **Intelligence**: Subtle integration of AI/tech elements without overwhelming tradition

## Content Voice & Tone
- **Educational**: Informative but never condescending
- **Respectful**: Honor cultural traditions and regional variations
- **Enthusiastic**: Share the joy and passion for momos
- **Precise**: Accurate information with proper citations
- **Inclusive**: Welcome all backgrounds and experience levels

## Application Guidelines
- Logo should maintain clear space equal to the height of one momo shape
- Primary colors should dominate, with secondary and accent colors used sparingly
- Typography hierarchy should be clear and consistent
- Images should be high-quality, appetizing, and culturally respectful
- AI elements should enhance, not replace, human cultural knowledge

## Digital Applications
- Website header, navigation, and footer
- Social media profiles and posts
- Documentation and technical materials  
- API interfaces and developer tools
- Mobile app interfaces
- Email templates and communications
