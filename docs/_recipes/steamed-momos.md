### Ingredients

- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup water (approx)
- 400g minced meat or mixed vegetables
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, grated
- Salt and pepper

### Directions

1. Make the dough by mixing flour and water until smooth. Rest for 30 minutes.
2. Prepare filling: combine minced meat/vegetables with onions, garlic, ginger, and seasoning.
3. Roll small dough circles, place filling, and seal.
4. Steam for 12–15 minutes until cooked.

Serve with a spicy chutney.
