## Overview

India's love affair with momos began in the northeastern hill stations — Darjeeling, Gangtok, Shillong — where Tibetan and Nepali communities had been making dumplings for generations. In the 1990s and 2000s, momos exploded across the Indian subcontinent, carried by migration and fuelled by the country's insatiable appetite for street food.

Today, India is the world's largest momo market by volume, with an estimated **2 billion momos consumed annually** across the country.

## Indian Momo Styles

India has taken the traditional momo and run with it, creating an entire category of Indo-Tibetan fusion dumplings:

| Style | Region | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Classic Steamed** | Northeast, Delhi | Closest to the original Nepali/Tibetan style |
| **Tandoori Momo** | Delhi, Punjab | Marinated in tandoori spices and grilled in a clay oven |
| **Afghani Momo** | Delhi street food | Drenched in a creamy, mildly spiced white sauce |
| **Kurkure (Crunchy) Momo** | Pan-India | Coated in a crispy batter and deep-fried |
| **Chilli Momo** | Pan-India | Stir-fried with Indo-Chinese chilli sauce and peppers |
| **Butter Momo** | North India | Momos tossed in a butter-garlic sauce |
| **Chocolate Momo** | Urban metros | Dessert momo filled with chocolate ganache |

## Regional Highlights

### Darjeeling & Sikkim
The heartland of Indian momos. Nearly identical to Nepali style — steamed, kothey, and fried with authentic Himalayan chutneys. Pork momos are a local speciality.

### Delhi NCR
India's momo capital by volume. The city's street vendors have innovated wildly — tandoori, afghani, and kurkure momos were all born here. Concentrated in areas like Majnu Ka Tilla (Tibetan colony), Amar Colony, and GT Road.

### Kolkata
Strong Tibetan-Chinese influence. Tangra (Kolkata's Chinatown) serves momos with sweet chilli and vinegar sauce. Pork and prawn fillings are common.

### Bangalore & Mumbai
Momos arrived later but spread rapidly through food trucks and delivery apps. Fusion varieties (pizza momo, paneer tikka momo) thrive here.
