## Introduction

The classic steamed momo is a dumpling native to the Himalayan region. This article covers traditional fillings, dough, and steaming techniques.

## Ingredients

- Dough: flour, water, salt
- Filling: minced meat or vegetables, onions, garlic, ginger, spices

## Preparation

1. Prepare the dough and rest for 30 minutes.
2. Mix the filling ingredients and season to taste.
3. Roll the dough, fill, and pleat.
4. Steam until cooked through.

Enjoy your momo!
