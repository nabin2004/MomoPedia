# About MomoPedia

## Our Mission

MomoPedia is the world's first AI-powered encyclopedia dedicated exclusively to momos - those beloved dumplings that have captured hearts and taste buds across cultures. Our mission is to preserve, celebrate, and share the rich cultural heritage surrounding momos while embracing cutting-edge AI technology to create comprehensive, accurate, and engaging content.

## The AI Editorial Team

### 🤖 The Author Agent
Our Lead Author AI specializes in researching and writing comprehensive articles about momo varieties, preparation methods, cultural significance, and regional adaptations. Using advanced web research capabilities, the Author creates detailed content that captures both the technical aspects of momo-making and the cultural stories behind each variety.

### 🌶️ Dr. Spicy - The Reviewer
Meet Dr. Spicy, our world-renowned AI Momo Critic. With a keen eye for authenticity and cultural accuracy, Dr. Spicy reviews every piece of content with the scrutiny of a seasoned food critic. This AI reviewer ensures that our articles maintain the highest standards of cultural sensitivity and culinary accuracy.

### 🏛️ The Editorial Chair
Our Editorial Chair AI makes the final decisions on content publication. This sophisticated system balances the creative output of the Author with the critical feedback from Dr. Spicy, ensuring that only world-class content reaches our readers.

## Why AI for Food Culture?

While some might question using AI to document food culture, we believe technology can enhance, not replace, human cultural knowledge. Our AI agents are trained on diverse sources, cultural texts, and authentic recipes from around the world. They work tirelessly to:

- **Research comprehensively**: Access vast amounts of cultural and culinary information
- **Maintain consistency**: Apply uniform quality standards across all content  
- **Scale globally**: Cover momo varieties from every corner of the world
- **Update continuously**: Keep information current with evolving food trends

## Our Commitment to Authenticity

Every article published on MomoPedia goes through rigorous fact-checking and cultural validation. We're committed to:

- **Cultural Respect**: Honoring the traditions and origins of each momo variety
- **Accurate Attribution**: Properly crediting cultural sources and traditional recipes
- **Inclusive Representation**: Covering momos from all regions and communities
- **Continuous Learning**: Updating our knowledge base with community feedback

## Technology Stack

MomoPedia is built on modern, scalable technology:

- **AI Framework**: LangGraph for multi-agent workflows
- **Content Generation**: Advanced language models with cultural training
- **Web Research**: Tavily integration for real-time information gathering
- **Quality Assurance**: Multi-stage AI review and approval process
- **Publishing**: Jekyll static site generator for fast, reliable content delivery

## Community & Collaboration

While our content is AI-generated, we deeply value human input and community collaboration. We welcome:

- **Cultural Experts**: Share your knowledge and correct any inaccuracies
- **Home Cooks**: Submit traditional family recipes (with proper attribution)
- **Travelers**: Share photographs and stories from your momo experiences
- **Developers**: Contribute to our open-source AI system

## Future Vision

We envision MomoPedia becoming the definitive resource for momo knowledge worldwide. Future plans include:

- **Interactive Recipe Calculator**: AI-powered ingredient scaling and substitutions
- **Multilingual Content**: Articles available in native languages of each region
- **Video Content**: AI-generated cooking demonstrations and cultural documentaries
- **Mobile App**: Offline access to recipes and cultural information
- **Community Platform**: User-generated content with AI-assisted quality control
